GDO710 Development Practice

Introduction

This first module is GDO710 Development Practice which is based around practice and reflection where I will be graded by this critical reflective journal and a case study video at the end of the module. This week is the first iteration of the first sprint and will focus on getting oriented onto the course by reviewing the course structure, understanding the first module in greater depth and reviewing how good academic practice can be developed. 

The posts I will write will document my prior and current notions of development practice and will show the research, reflection and deliberate practices I have endeavoured. 
By the end of this module I will have gained the confidence and clarity to state clearly my aspirations after graduating from the course, what skills I need to improve on in order to achieve these aspirations and what medium I need to create to evidence my skillset.
