About Us

Fine Chap Games is a newly established indie games development company comprising of two members. We've only released one game so far on the google play store but we plan on releasing several more inovative and enjoyable games in the near future. We mainly develop 2D games but we have plans to venture into 3D development eventually. Find our portfolios and CVs below if you are looking to hire.

The Team

Connor Orton BSc

Games Programmer

Connor is a games programmer. He has been passionate about games production since an early age who started out using RPG Maker XP to create 2D RPG games. His responsibilities include designing and writing game concept and systems, overseeing the project pipeline and asset implementation in the game engine. He is currently studying a masters degree at Falmouth University whilst seeking employment.

Natalie is a 2D digital artist. She has always been passionate about art and being creative since her first drawing as a child.
