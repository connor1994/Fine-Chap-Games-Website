About Us

Fine Chap Games is a newly established indie games development company comprising of two members who are a couple. We have released a single game so far on the google play store and we have many ideas and games to be released in the near future. We mainly develop 2D pixel art style games but we have plans to venture into 3D development eventually.

Game Projects

Pixel Pinball Arcade

Pixel Pinball Arcade is a 2D pixel art pinball game for Android created in Unity and written in C#. It was released in 2019 by Fine Chap Games and self published to the google play store. A link to the google play store page can be found here: Pixel Pinball Arcade
