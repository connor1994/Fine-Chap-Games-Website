About Us

Fine Chap Games is a newly established indie games development company comprising of two members. We've only released one game so far on the google play store but we plan on releasing several more inovative and enjoyable games in the near future. We mainly develop 2D games but we have plans to venture into 3D development eventually. Find our portfolios and CVs below if you are looking to hire.

The Team

Connor Orton BSC

Game Designer & Programmer

Connor is a games designer and programmer. He has been passionate about games production since an early age who started out using RPG Maker 2000 to create 2D RPG games. His responsibilities include designing and writing game systems in a variety of different programming languages. He also oversees the project pipeline and asset implementation in the game engine. 

Portfolio & CV

Natalie Smith

2D Artist

Natalie is a 2D digital artist. She has always been passionate about art and never forgets the day she got her first art tablet and pen. Her responsibilities include conceptualizing various game ideas and creating assets based on them using industry standard software and art techniques. She has experience using the latest art programs such as Photoshop, Aseprite, Paint Tool SAI and more.
